// THE PROJECT

It began as a signal.
It became an obligation.

In the dossiers it's called Project Rootsapiens — an attempt to answer a single question that the surface world was never told had been asked. The signal didn't come from the stars. It came from underneath us. Every scientist who heard it interpreted it differently. All of them agreed on one thing: it knew they were coming.
